Privacy Policy — 7BTVFZ Emote Picker
Last updated: March 2026

The 7BTVFZ Emote Picker extension for Twitch does not collect, store, use, or transmit any personal user data.
The only data that is saved is the list of favorite emotes.
It is stored exclusively locally in the user's browser using chrome.storage.local and is never sent to any servers, third parties, or the developer.
The extension operates completely offline regarding the storage and processing of your preferences.
Enjoy a comfortable usage experience.
Best wishes to all.
